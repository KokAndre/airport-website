Aviation Meets Community at Tedderfield Airpark

Tedderfield Airpark offers a limited number of exclusive hangar spaces for sale, providing private pilots and aviation professionals the opportunity to base themselves in a vibrant, aviation-centered community.

Our hangars aren't just spaces - they're access points to a full aviation lifestyle.

From early-morning takeoffs to the camaraderie shared over evening landings, Tedderfield is where your aviation dreams come to life.

Why Invest in a Hangar at Tedderfield?

Located at one of South Africa's premier private airfields, Tedderfield Airpark provides safe, secure, and spacious hangars designed for both private and commercial aviation needs.

As a hangar owner, you'll join a community of passionate aviators, enjoy access to fly-ins, fly-aways, and other social events.

Tedderfield also offers maintenance facilities, an active social calendar, and a host of amenities that make your aviation life smooth and enjoyable.

Our hangar spaces are in high demand, attracting everyone from seasoned pilots to aviation enthusiasts who want a home for their aircraft.
