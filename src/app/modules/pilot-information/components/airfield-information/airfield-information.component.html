Airfield Highlights

Our Little Slice of Aviation Paradise: Nestled in Johannesburg South, Tedderfield Airpark started life as a peach farm and has since evolved into a buzzing, vibrant airfield that offers so much. And yes, we've kept a little of that peach-farm charm.

A Runway with a View: Runway 11/29 may not have the longest stretch in the world, but it more than makes up for it with unbeatable views and a smooth, well-maintained surface. Just watch out for the occasional crosswind gust - a Tedderfield trademark. And don't forget to wave at the clubhouse on your downwind leg! Well maybe not!

Flying In:

Circuit Patterns and Joining Procedures: At Tedderfield, we like to keep it straightforward with simple circuits unless otherwise noted. We recommend you check out our joining and landing procedures - taking in a wide view of the airfield to make sure you're aware of any fellow aviators in the area. (Pro tip: Everyone appreciates a good lookout!)

Wind Socks and Local Wildlife: Keep an eye on the wind socks, and yes they are fickle and often indicate differing directions simultaneously. If you see a furry friend roaming nearby - don't worry, we're on it. Still, a quick check for feathered and furred traffic on finals won't hurt.

Landing Tips:

Grease it Down, Please! We take pride in keeping our landings as smooth as possible, not only for your comfort but also to keep our neighbours happy. (And yes, we can all hear that last-minute power-up!) Let's leave the corkscrews and cowboy landings for the aerobatic clubs and aim to keep it classy.

Traffic Awareness: Be mindful of who's around and what they're up to. Our radio etiquette is light-hearted but polite. If you can keep chatter to a minimum and convey essentials (without too much sass), everyone benefits.

On the Ground:

Parking and Tiedowns: Prime parking awaits! Secure your aircraft in the best spots for weather protection and proximity to clubhouse amenities. Look out for the signage, along the northern side of the tarred runway. Don't be shy to ask about anything.

Navigating Tedderfield Rules

Friendly Warning on Tailgating: We take security seriously, so don't try sneaking in behind someone else. There's a reason our WAR Security team is so well-named.

Aviation Traditions and Events

The Famous Fly-Ins: Our fly-ins have reached legendary status, from pancake breakfast fly-ins to the epic lemon meringue bake-off. Keep an eye on the events calendar on our web site and bring your best competitive spirit.

Club Events and Socials: Braais, Halloween spookfests, and summer socials - the best way to meet other pilots and share a few stories over a cold one. (Pro tip: the club's unofficial motto is "The more, the merrier!")
