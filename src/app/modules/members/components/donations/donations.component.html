Why Should You Contribute?
  • Be the Rockstar: Want your name to shine brighter than the runway lights? This is your backstage pass to airpark fame.
  • FOMO is Real.

Cash, a piece of equipment, your time, your expertise, or maybe even just a really great idea (we'll count it). Think of it as your ticket to airpark immortality.

Simple! Just reach out by making contact on the website. We'll handle the rest - and promise your name will shine as brightly as our runway lights.
