Welcome to the Tedderfield Airpark Members Only Portal

As a valued member of Tedderfield Airpark, you have access to exclusive content, updates, and resources designed to enhance your experience within our aviation community. By signing in, you'll gain access to a range of benefits tailored just for our members.

Why Sign In?

By logging into the members-only portal, you'll unlock the following benefits:

  • Exclusive Access to Hangar and Stand Listings: Be the first to view available hangars and stands before they're listed elsewhere. Secure your ideal spot within the airpark.
  • Community News & Updates: Stay up-to-date with the latest news, Trustee decisions, and upcoming events at Tedderfield Airpark.
  • Event Invitations: Receive priority invitations to fly-ins, social gatherings, and other special events organised for our airpark community.
  • Private Documents and Reports: Access essential documents such as the latest airpark meeting minutes, financial reports, and operational updates, all in one convenient location.
  • Maintenance & Service Requests: Submit requests for maintenance or services directly to our management team, ensuring that your needs are attended to promptly
  • Special Offers & Discounts: Take advantage of exclusive offers, including discounted rates for clubhouse bookings, maintenance services, and special event sponsorships.
